Q: What is ThummimAI?
A: ThummimAI is a learning and medication assistance platform that helps users schedule their medication reminders, ask questions, and get personalized help.

Q: How do I add a medication reminder?
A: You can tell the AI “Remind me to take [medication] at [time]” and it will set it up for you automatically.

Q: Can ThummimAI replace a doctor?
A: No. ThummimAI is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor for diagnosis, prescription, and medical decisions.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. All your reminders and conversation logs are stored securely. We follow standard encryption and data protection practices.
